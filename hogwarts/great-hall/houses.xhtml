Hogwarts has four houses in which students can be divided. The houses are named after the four founders of Hogwarts;
Godric Gryffindor, Rowena Ravenclaw, Salazar Slytherin and Helga Hufflepuff. Each house has it's own colors, weapon and treats.
Below a small summary about each House is given.

Gryffindor

Gryffindor, the House named after Godric Gryffindor, one of the founders of Hogwarts, is one of the houses of Hogwarts.
The colors of Gryffindor are red and gold, with a golden lion on it's banners as a weapon. The Founder's object of Gryffindor, on display in the Headmaster's Office, is the sword of Godric Gryffindor, with which it is said he slayed many beasts threatening the school.
The dormitory of Gryffindor is on the 7th floor, under the Astronomy tower and is guarded by the portrait of the Fat Lady, to whom must be told the password only known by members of Gryffindor. Only the House's Ghost, Nearly-headless Nick, is allowed entrance. Minverva McGonagall is the House respondible teacher.
It is said students in Gryffindor are brave, without nerve, have courage and chivalry and are very daring.
Some famous members are [[../hall-of-fame/harry-potter|Harry Potter]], [[../hall-of-fame/ron-weasley|Ron Weasley]], [[../hall-of-fame/hermione-granger|Hermione Granger]], Minverva McGonagall, Remus Lupin, Sirius Black, Rubeus Hagrid and Neville Longbottom.

Slytherin

Slytherin, the House named after Salazar Slytherin, one of the founders of Hogwarts, is one of the houses of Hogwarts.
The colors of Slytherin are green and silver, with a silver serpent on it's banners as a weapon. The Founder's object of Slytherin, on display in the Headmaster's Office, is the medaillon of Salazar Slytherin, which has powers to block curses.
The dormitory of Slytherin is in the basement, next to the potions. The password for entrance needs to be told to stone wall. The location and password are only known to the students of Slytherin. Only the House's Ghost, the bloody baron, is allowed entrance.
Severus Snape is the House responsible.
It is said students in Hufflepuff are resourcefull, cunning, ambitious, determined, self-preserved and clever.
Some famous members are Merlin, Severus Snape, Draco Malfoy, Tom Marvalo Riddle and Bellatrix Lastrange.

Ravenclaw

Ravenclaw, the House named after Rowena Ravenclaw, one of the founders of Hogwarts, is one of the houses of Hogwarts.
The colors of Ravenclaw are blue and silver, with a silver raven on it's banners as a weapon. The Founder's object of Ravenclaw, on display in the Headmaster's Office, is the tiara of Rowena Ravenclaw, which grants the user great wisdom.
The dormitory of Ranvenclaw is on the 5th floor, behind a door without a keyhole or doorknob, only with a knocker in the form of raven, to which the answer to a riddle must be told to get in. Only the House's Ghost, The Grey Lady, is allowed entrance. Filius Flitwick is the House responsible. It is said students in Ravenclaw are intelligent, witty, wise, creative, original and very accepting.
Some famous members are Filius Flitwick, Luna Lovegood, Cho Chang, Gilderoy Lockheart and Garrick Ollivander.

Gryffindor

Hufflepuff, the House named after Helga Hufflepuff, one of the founders of Hogwarts, is one of the houses of Hogwarts.
The colors of Hufflepuff are yellow and black, with a black badger on it's banners as a weapon. The Founder's object of Gryffindor, on display in the Headmaster's Office, is the cup of Helga Hufflepuff, which has healing powers to whomever drinks from it.
The dormitory of Hufflepuff is in the basement next to the kitchen. A rhythm must be knocked to gain entrance. Only the House's Ghost, the Fat Frier, is allowed entrance. Pomona Sprout is the House responsible.
It is said students in Hufflepuff are dedicated, work hard, play fair, are patient, kind and tolerant and very loyal.
Some famous members are Pomona Sprout, Newt Scamander, Nymphadora Tonks, Cedric Diggory and Teddy Lupin.

Each house also provides students with sleeping quarters in the form of [[../basic/provisions/dormitories|dormitories]].
