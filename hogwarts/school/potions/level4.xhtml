Potion 1: Wideye Potion

This potion can be used by wizards who have troubles keeping awake. This potion prevents the drinker from falling asleep. It also can be used to awake persons from drugging or concussion.
The difficulty level of this potion is beginner.

Ingredients:

  • 6 snake fangs
  • 4 measures of Standard Ingredient
  • 6 dried Billywig stings
  • 2 sprigs of Wolfsbane
The following steps need to be taken to brew this potion:
  1. Add 6 snake fangs to the mortar
  2. Add 4 measures of Standard Ingredient to the mortar
  3. Add 6 dried Billywig stings to your cauldron
  4. Heat on a medium temperature for 30 seconds
  5. Finely crush the ingredients in the mortar
  6. Add 4 measures of the crushed ingredients to your cauldron
  7. Stir 3 times, clockwise
  8. Wave your wand
  9. Leave to brew and return in 8 hours.
  10. Add 2 sprigs of Wolfsbane to your cauldron
  11. Stir 3 times, anti-clockwise
  12. Wave your wand to complete this potion
