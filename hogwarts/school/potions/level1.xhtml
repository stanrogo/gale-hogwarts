In this course, you will learn basic recipes, ingredients and incantations which can be used to brew different potions.

Information about the Potion course

The subject is obligated for all first year to fifth year students, and can be continued as N.E.W.T. course in the sixth and seventh year. In order to be able to learn the advanced courses in the sixth and seventh year, a student needs to pass his O.W.L. examination. A standard potion kit includes plants (such as Belladonna) and supplies (such as glass phials and weighing scales).

This class is tought in the dungeons of Hogwarts.
