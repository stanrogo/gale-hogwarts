Teachers

Known professors of Potion class are all male. The oldest known teachers are Swoopstikes and Vindictus Viridian.
Professor Horace Slughorn has tought this class for several years: from 1942 - 1981 and from 1996 onwards. The teacher who replaced him in the meantime was [[../basic/headmasters|Severus Snape]]. Slughorn was known for his lenient way of teaching; Snape however had the reputation to be very strict, especially for students who wanted to take the advanced classes.
