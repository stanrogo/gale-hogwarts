Required Materials

The following books are obliged to buy before following this course:

  • The Dark Forces: A Guide to Self-Protection, by Quentin Trimble
    obliged for first and second year students
  • Dark Arts Defence: Basics for Beginners
    obliged for first and second year students
  • The Essential Defence Against the Dark Arts, by Arsenius Jigger
    obliged for third, fourth and fifth year students
  • Defensive Magical Theory, by Wilbert Slinkhard
    obliged for third, fourth and fifth year students
  • Confronting the Faceless, N.E.W.T. edition
    obliged for sixth and seventh year students
In the year of Gildery Lockhart the following books, written by himself, were also obligated:
  • Break With a Banshee
  • Gadding with Ghouls
  • Holidays with Hags
  • Travels with Trolls
  • Voyages with Vampires
  • Wandering with Werewolves
  • Year with a Yeti
Furthermore, the student is expected to bring the following equipment to class:
  • Wand
  • Parchment
  • Quill
