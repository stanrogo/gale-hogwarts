Spell 1: Mimble wimble

This friendly spell will bind the target's tongue to keep him or her talking about a specific subject. This feature also allows one to prevent the victim from incanting.

The light that the wand should emit is blue, and the hand movement can be best described as following the clock from 12 to 3 clockwise, then going up to the number 12 and again follow the 'curve' until you reached the number 7.
