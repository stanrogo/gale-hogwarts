As the wizarding world is full of hazards and dark magic, students learn how to protect themselves in dangerous situations. They do not only learn how to magically defend themselves against dark creaturs, dark arts and other dark charms, they also learn offensive magic. This last will be tought in a duel, which requirese both offensive as defensive spells.

Information about the Defence against the Dark Arts course

The subject is obligated for all first year to fifth year students, and can be continued as N.E.W.T. course in the sixth and seventh year. In order to be able to learn the advanced courses in the sixth and seventh year, a student needs to pass his O.W.L. examination. The lessons generally have a practical approach, with many of the teachers believing that when it comes to the dark arts, a practical approach is the best way. Next to practical lessons in class, students need to make theoretical assignments as homework. Defence Against the Dark Arts classes take place on the first-floor, class 104 and classroom 3C on the third floor.
