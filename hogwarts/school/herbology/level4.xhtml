Plant 1: Mandragora, or Mandrake

This plant has a root which has the shape that looks like a baby. The cry of a young mandragora will cause the hearer to faint, where the cry of a mature examplar can be fatal. To stop the plant from screaming, it should be earthed. The process of becoming a mature mandragora resembles the aging in humans: mandragors can become moody, secretive, loud and even have acne.
The leaves of a mandragora are green/bownish and the plant can be used as ingredient in Restorative Draught, to cure petrified creatures.
