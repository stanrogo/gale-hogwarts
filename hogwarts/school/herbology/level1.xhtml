This course will teach you all about the different magical and mundane plants and fungi. You will learn how to care and utilise plants, learn about their magical properties and what they are used for. Many plants can be used for potions or medicine.

Information about the Herbology course

The subject is obligated for all first year to fifth year students, and can be continued as N.E.W.T. course in the sixth and seventh year. In order to be able to learn the advanced courses in the sixth and seventh year, a student needs to pass his O.W.L. examination.
This class is given in the greenhouses, located behind the castle. Depending on the students' year, the class will be given in a different greenhouse.
