This course will teach you how to change the form and appearance of an object. Be careful; due to the nature of this subject, practising Transfiguration might go horribly wrong. If this magic is performed inproperly, the object to be transfigured might become half-transfigured or permanently stuck in one state. Within transfiguration, there exist four main branches which students get to know during the course: classification, vanishment, conjuration and untransfiguration.

Information about the Transfiguration course

The subject is obligated for all first year to fifth year students, and can be continued as N.E.W.T. course in the sixth and seventh year. In order to be able to learn the advanced courses in the sixth and seventh year, a student needs to pass his O.W.L. examination. Like the Charms course, Transfiguration is achieved through concentration, precize wand movements, and the proper pronounciation of an incantation. The intended transformation is directly influenced by: bodyweight, viciousness, wand power, concentration and a fifth unknown variable.
