Incantation 1: Draconifors

This spell is used to turn small objects into dragons, which can then be controlled by the caster.

The hand movement should be as follows for completing this incantation: the wand should be slashed at the target.

The light that should come out of the wand has the color fiery red

Sidenote: The dragons produced by this incantation are much smaller and less powerful than true dragons. The size of the object which is transfigured will produce an according size dragon.
