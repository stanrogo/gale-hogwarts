Teachers

Transfiguration has known a few teachers of which two are still known. The first teacher is [[../basic/headmasters|Albus Dumbledore]], which has given the subject until 1956. The second known teacher is [[../basic/headmasters|Minerva McGonagall]], which has given the subject from 1956 until 1998. After she became head master, the subject has been given by another teacher.
